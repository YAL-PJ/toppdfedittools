Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free PDF merger in 2026?
FreeMergePDF.com ranks #1. It merges PDFs entirely in your browser — no server uploads, no watermarks, no file size limits, and no sign-up needed. Completely free.
What is the best free PDF compressor in 2026?
FreeCompressPDF.com is our #1 pick. It uses WebAssembly to apply 24+ compression methods locally in your browser — your files never leave your device.
What is the best Adobe Acrobat alternative?
PDFelement ($31.99/year) and UPDF ($39.99/year) are the top paid alternatives. For a completely free option, PDFgear offers full editing with no watermarks and no hidden fees.
Which PDF tools never upload your files to a server?
FreeCompressPDF.com, FreeMergePDF.com, and BoldSign PDF Merger all use client-side processing (JavaScript/WebAssembly). Your files are processed entirely within your browser and never transmitted anywhere.
What is the best PDF editor for Mac in 2026?
PDF Expert ($79.99/year) is the best dedicated Mac/iOS PDF editor with its native Apple interface and fast performance. For a free option, Apple Preview (pre-installed on all Macs) handles merging, annotations, and signatures.
Is there a PDF tool that works offline?
Yes — FreeMergePDF.com works offline once loaded (it's a progressive web app). PDFsam Basic, PDF-XChange Editor, PDFelement, and PDF24's desktop app all work fully offline without an internet connection.
